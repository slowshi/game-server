Two players look to trade and sell their goods to become the richest trader.

Round Ends
Only 3 goods left or No draw Cards

Round winner
Person who gets the most gold that rounds gets a token

Game winner
Person to get to two tokens

Setup
1. Center of table has 3 Camels
2. Each player gets 5 cards face down. Camels get put directly onto the table.
3. Two cards places face up from top of deck.

On Player Turn
Take Cards
Take ONE good from table and replace it from one in the top of the deck
Take MORE than one good from the table you MUST trade the exact number from your hand/herd
Take ALL of the camels from the center and put them in your herd then replace the cards with cards from the deck.

Sell Cards
Sell as many cards of one good as you would like. You gain one token for every card you gain.
Selling 3x 4x or 5x cards of one good gains a bonus token
You must sell at least 2 goods if they are expensive goods
